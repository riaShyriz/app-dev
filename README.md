# How to Make Millions Before My Grandma Dies

**How to Make Millions Before My Grandma Dies** is a Thai family drama film that beautifully blends humor, emotion, and life lessons about love and priorities.

## Main Characters
- M (Putthipong Assaratanakul)
- Amah (Usha Seamkhum)
- Mui
- Mom (M’s mother)

## Plot Summary
> *“Sometimes, what we’re chasing isn’t money — it’s time.”*

M is a young man who tries to get close to his rich grandmother, Amah, hoping to inherit her wealth. But as he spends more time with her, he learns the true value of family, culture, and compassion.

## Memorable Themes
- Family over fortune
- Life is short — cherish your loved ones
- The meaning of true wealth

## Why I Recommend It 🌟
This film hits hard emotionally. It made me laugh and cry, especially in scenes where Amah shows her love in subtle, touching ways.  
It’s a reminder to love our grandparents while we still can.
